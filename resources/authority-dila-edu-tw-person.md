# DILA Person Authority Database 人名規範資料庫

**URL:** https://authority.dila.edu.tw/person/

**Language:** Chinese

**Region:** Taiwan

## Summary

The DILA Person Authority Database is part of the Buddhist Studies Authority Database Project maintained by Dharma Drum Institute of Liberal Arts. It provides normalized person records for figures relevant to Buddhist texts and Buddhist studies, including variant names and identifiers used across DILA and CBETA-linked resources.

The site offers search and browsing views for person authority records. Its public landing/search pages list most-referenced people, recently added records, recently updated records, and people with many alternative names, making it useful for entity disambiguation, prosopography, text annotation, and linked-data work in Buddhist studies.
