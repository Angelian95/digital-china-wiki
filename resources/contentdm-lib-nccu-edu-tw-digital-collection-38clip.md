# 民國38年前重要剪報資料庫

**URL:** https://contentdm.lib.nccu.edu.tw/digital/collection/38clip

**Language:** Chinese

**Region:** Taiwan

## Summary

This NCCU Digital Archives collection provides access to important newspaper clippings from before ROC year 38 (1949). The collection is hosted in CONTENTdm and can be browsed directly or searched through the platform's search and advanced-search interfaces.

The resource is relevant for research on Republican-era newspapers, political and social history, and the preservation of newspaper-derived primary sources in Taiwan library collections. The public interface includes collection-level navigation, browsing, and a traditional Chinese display option.
